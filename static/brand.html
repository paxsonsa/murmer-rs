Concept

Signal propagation through a mesh

The mark represents actors as hollow nodes — self-contained, stateful processes — connected by message paths. Concentric ripples radiate outward from each node: the murmur of messages propagating through the system. Whether two nodes are millimeters apart in-process or kilometers apart over QUIC, the ripples look the same. Location transparency, visualized.

The warm gradient (amber → coral) nods to the signal warmth of the BEAM — where the inspiration began — while the dark ground and precise geometry anchor it firmly in Rust's performance-first identity.
